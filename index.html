About

International Conference on Biomaterials, Bio-Design and Manufacturing (BDMC 2023) is organized by Zhejiang University and University of Minho and co-organized by University College London, Universidad de Las Palmas de Gran Canaria and Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital.The BDMC 2023 conference focus on novel research, novel technology and novel applications in bionic design and manufacturing.

Topics

Topic1: Biomaterials

Topic2: 3D-Bioprinting

Topic3: Bionic Design

Topic4: Bioelectronics and Devices

Topic5: Organoids and Organ-on-a-chip

Topic6: Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine

BAMOS: Biomaterials and Additive Manufacturing: Osteochondral Scaffold Innovation
