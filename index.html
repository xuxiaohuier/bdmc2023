About

 BDMC 2023 is organized by Zhejiang University and University of Minho and co-organized by University College London, Universidad de Las Palmas de Gran Canaria and Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital.The BDMC 2023 conference focus on novel research, novel technology and novel applications in bionic design and manufacturing.
